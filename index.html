POCKET-MASTER

Having access to a financial savings tool makes it possible to access;

  • a pool of capital in case of emergencies or
  • save money for a large purchase.
  • In case of unforeseen illness, or crisis such as the current ; CoronaVirus, members can rely on their group members and the resulting group fund to quickly take out a loan.

By saving as a group, the lower income people can help each other learn basic financial skills. As a group, they can more easily receive literacy and money management training from group promoters or trainers from NGOs, and also learn from other more literate members. Hence, the ‘POCKET-MASTER YOUTH INITIATIVE’. With Pocket-master, youth are able to access emergency funds as well as capital for small business via small monthly contributions. For as little as Kes.1000 monthly(per person), for six months, a group can get capital for their business.
